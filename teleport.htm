Teleport( <position>, <angles> )

Module: AI
SP Only

Summary:

Teleport the actor to a new position with the given origin and angles.



Call this on:

<actor> An actor



Example:

 self Teleport( org, angles );



Required Args:

  • 1 : <position> (point) The actor's new position.
  • 2 : <angles> (vector) The actor's new angles.
