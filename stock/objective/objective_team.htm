Objective_Team( <objective_number>, <team> )

Module: Objective
MP Only

Summary:

Sets the team that the objective is for. Allows having different objectives for each team



Example:

Objective_Team( 0, "allies" );



Required Args:

  • 1 : <objective_number> The ID of the objective to alter
  • 2 : <team> The team that the objective is for. Valid entries are 'allies', 'axis', or 'none'
