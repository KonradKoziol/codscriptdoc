SetHoverParams( <radius>, <speed>, <accel> )

Module: Vehicles

Summary:

Set the hovering parameters.



Call this on:

a vehicle



Example:

self SetHoverParams( 50, 1, 0.5 );



Required Args:

  • 1 : <radius> The hovering range.


Optional Args

  • 1 : <speed> Speed to hover at (MPH)
  • 2 : <accel> Acceleration to hover at (MPH/s)
