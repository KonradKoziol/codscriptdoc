SetTurningAbility( <turning ability> )

Module: Vehicles

Summary:

If this is higher, helicopters can make sharper turns to match goal positions better.



Call this on:

a vehicle



Example:

self SetTurningAbility( 0.9 );



Required Args:

  • 1 : <turning ability> Between 0.001 and 1.0. Defaults to 0.5
